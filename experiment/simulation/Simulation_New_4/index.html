Instructions:

  1. Before starting the process read the instructions completely
  2. Select the Select Arc Wire Diameter (in mm) of a value between 1.2 and 1.6 from the dropdown list.
  3. Click on Next button
  4. Choose the Arc Voltage (in Volts) of any Real Value between 20 and 30
  5. Click on Next button
  6. Choose the Arc Current (in Ampere) of any Real Value between 180 and 360
  7. Click on Submit button
  8. Choose the Welding Speed (in mm/min) of any Real Value between 25 and 41
  9. Click on Submit button
  10. Observe the Welding simulation starts and it shows the final welded portion.
  11. Observe the plot and hover the mouse on plot for the weld bead dimensions i.e., reinforcement height, penetrationdepth, and bead width obtained, for the chosen weld parameters

      12. Note:
    • Use scroll button to change the value of the knob
    • After Simulation is over, click on "Stop" button to restart the experiment
